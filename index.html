Visualize Digital Twin and CAD Projects for Augmented and Virtual Reality presentations

With ARCAD Editor you can import and create animations of exploded views and parts highlights.

Documentation

Free version: 0.1 alpha - March 7 2019

Viewer

Visualize your animations, show your desings and ideias in a different way.
Click in the "Marker" button to download the AR marker.

You are in the Mobile version, so you only have access to the Viewer, to get full access use a desktop.

Editor

The Editor allows you to make animations with the 3D models.
Move, rotate or change the scale, and then set a new key frame.
Key frames are the steps of your animations, they represent the state of the 3D model. Supported formats: .OBJ, .arcad.
